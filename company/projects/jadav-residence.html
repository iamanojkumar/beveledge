Jadav Residence

Jadav Residence is a 8000 sq ft villa in Bengaluru designed by Lasya Umesh, principal designer of Beveledge Studio, embodies a sophisticated India-modern aesthetic, blending meaningful interior spaces with thoughtful inclusion of outdoor spaces.

Location:
Nagarbhavi, Bengaluru
Project Type:
Villa
Square feet:
8,200

The adjoining dining area maintains a chic palette of gray and black, accentuated with touches of brass for an understated luxury amidst the mutedbackgrounds of the living space furniture.

A modest staircase leads to the first floor, where there is a double-height living room, crowned with a custom chandelier, which merges modern design with subtle Indian elements in the surrounding.

Upon entry, the ground floor offers a multifunctional parking area that transforms into an event-ready space,with a beautiful ceiling design as accent. Bedrooms are styled with bold colors that evoke style, comfort and sophistication, while the bathrooms reflect a modern, luxe aesthetic.
