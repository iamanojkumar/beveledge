Maison 325

Placed over the textured rug carrying slivers of the dominant hues, the central coffee table comprises a gorgeous bone inlay and brass-based table sourced from Udaipur and a customised gilded and veneered-top table which hints at the coming together of the old and new.

Location:
Bengaluru
Project Type:
Resedential
Year:
2023

The dapper, nested accent tables host grey marble tops, reiterating the plush materiality of the space.Tones of blue, lavender, and yellow bleed into the artwork, a bespoke creation of Artist Shraddha Rathi. Upping the opulence in a nook, the silver crystal pendant makes an unmissable cameo!

The blueprint of the living area organically expands with the inclusion of twin bar cabinets standing gracefully off to a side. Created in a taupe tone, the unit’s fascia is embellished with concentric circular embossing positioned over golden geometric bases.

An all-white marble pooja room has the most intricate details that we pored over! A white marble backdrop was chiselled to perfection to hold the mother of pearl and Swarovski Om symbol at its centre. Layers of the Vietnam marble were sculpted to create a wave-like pattern emanating from the Om symbol. We created this visual to depict spiritual vibrations originating from the holy symbol, highlighting a sense of meditative calm. The glass doors have been detailed painstakingly with gold hardware and a 'Tree of Life' set of brass handles.

Spelling stated luxury, the ensuite master bedroom is crafted to wholeheartedly represent the vision the clients have dreamed up! An ivory hue takes over the room, played up with colour to create bespoke moments. The dark emerald tufted headboard is one such addition of colour, rested against the wall’s wainscoting. The deep veneer-covered nightstands complement the artwork, distilling the colour palette immaculately.

The focal headboard combines silver and purple-clad wallpapered panels, light grey fabric, and wood creating an impactful visual while anchoring functionality. The fabric bed and wall-mounted white nightstands extend from this backdrop, peering into the adjoining deck. The embroidered feather-patterned curtains and light grey sheers draw light softly into the resting space.

A quest to craft elegant residences, meticulously tailored to each client’s imagination, needs, and style.
