The Home Lounge

An open courtyard lush with greenery welcomes you as you step out from the entertainment lounge, effortlessly blending the indoors with the outdoors. The sleek staircase leads to the terrace, with its clean lines contrasting against the vibrant plants. The subtle difference in wooden flooring marks the boundary between the two spaces while maintaining visual harmony.

Location:
Bengaluru
Project Type:
Resedential
Year:
2023

This space embodies the essence of open-plan living, offering cozy sit-outs in the informal outdoor seating area for casual conversations, while the bar counter inside provides an immersive view of nature striking a perfect balance between luxury and relaxation, promoting a calm connection to the outdoors.

In the lounge, the bar counter takes center stage, emitting a chic and luxe vibe with its custom design. The backlit onyx surface adds a sophisticated glow, beautifully contrasting with the metallic brown backdrop that features hints of gold and black. The textured onyx is framed by a stunning back bar, where suspended metal bars elegantly hold the shelves, creating a sense of lightness and structure. Adorning the space is a sleek black drop light with charming bird accessories, adding a playful touch to the dramatic setting. Amidst all this opulence, the onyx stone bar counter stands out as the focal point.

The staircase outside the entertainment lounge leads to a custom-designed terrace, featuring a structure made from wooden rafters. These rafters not only provide structural integrity but also incorporate lighting, ensuring both practicality and aesthetic appeal.

This semi covered terrace area is divided into distinct areas for seating and a jacuzzi, marked by floor demarcations and varying levels. As you step onto the terrace, the design clearly defines the seating area and an elevated jacuzzi space next to it.

Surrounded by lush greenery, the terrace offers a tranquil retreat that makes one forget the bustling city beyond. The terrace feels like a natural extension of the entertainment lounge, creating a seamless indoor-outdoor experience that enhances relaxation and socialising.
